About me

A quick demo of using jQuery for par­al­lax. This example just cre­ates a nice visual effect but it could eas­ily be con­ver­ted for more con­struct­ive pur­poses but I’ll leave that up to you. Par­al­lax (for the sake of this post) is the effect whereby things at dif­fer­ent dis­tances move at dif­fer­ent rates, for example look­ing out of a train win­dow the closer items are the faster they move past you. This is as hor­ribly sim­pli­fied and inac­cur­ate descrip­tion but will suf­fice for the sake of this example. I’ve used three trans­par­ent PNGs for this but you can use as many lay­ers as you like or eas­ily replace the PNGs with div tags con­tain­ing whatever con­tent you need.
